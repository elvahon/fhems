OUR SERVICES

Fredie Hung Ethnic Minorities Service (FHEMS) is initiated in Spring 2020 to connect and allocate relevant social resources and community network, to enhance accessibility to information and knowledge of civil society, to magnify their participation in Hong Kong as citizens.

FEATURE NEWS & EVENTS

7th Batch Masks & Cleaning Kits Distribution

On this Friday and Saturday ( 19th & 20th March), FHEMS has dustributed 1,800 masks, 72 bags of cleaning kits with bleach, detergents and alcohol sprays to ethnic minorities in Yau Tsim Mong District.
